Hoi An is a familiar name to most of tourists once coming to visit Viet Nam - a
S-shaped country with red flat and yellow star. Hoi An is an old town full or charming beauties and antique architecture. And it is a truly paradise hidden under its tranquility.
Nowadays, the town attracts a lot of tourists visiting. Beside that, who want to relax , it's a fantastic place. We believe that the tour will bring you many unforgettable experiences!

Details:

Pickup

Pick up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An ) and move to the Hoi An Old Town.

Action

Take a stroll the old town, visit and learn about the covered The Japanese Covered Bridge; The Assembly Hall; The Old House of Tan Ky ; Hoi An Traditional Art Performance House and many Buddhist pagodas on the side.

Action

Visit Hoi An markets and purchase goods made by the town's inhabitants.
Enjoyed Banh Mi or Cao Lau (Hoi An signature food )
Finish tour .
